We are more than a Process Service company, we are an Attorney Service business providing Licensed Process Service, Skip-tracing, Court Filing, and Investigative services. Our services fully adhere to the rules and regulations of the Department of Consumer and Worker Protection and all Civil Practice, Real Property, and Family Laws pertaining to the service of process. Our servers utilities GPS-tracking and time-stamping applications on the job and provide bulletproof affidavits to ease your mind in your case.
Get to know the owner.
Notification Services has offices in Long Island and Queens, New York, and is well-suited to provide licensed process service in the Five Boroughs, Long Island, and internationally. We provide Skip Tracing, and filing of papers in all New York Courts.
Whether you’re a sole practitioner or an international firm, Notification Services can provide you with the results you need to locate and identify your adversaries and serve process.
Notification Services began as a small, accountable and diligent service company in 2011. Consistent professionalism and growth in the field has allowed us to become a trusted source for attorneys and process serving professionals. In 2022, founder Andrew Mega was elected to the Board of Directors of the New York State Professional Process Server’s Association where he serves on a Legislative Committee focused on advocating for Professional Process Servers in New York State.
